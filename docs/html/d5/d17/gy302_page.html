GY30/GY302

GY30 and GY302 modules are based on BH1750FVI that is an digital Ambient Light Sensor IC for IÂ²C bus interface. This IC is most suitable to obtain the ambient light data for adjusting LCD and Keypad backlight power of mobile phones. It is possible to detect wide range at high resolution (1 .. 65535 lux).

Key features are listed below, BH1750FVI details can be found into datasheet:

  • Power supply 3.3V - 5V;
  • I2C interface;
  • 16 bits ADC;
  • 1 - 65535 lux;
  • Spectral responsibility is approximately human eye response;
  • 50Hz/60Hz light noise reject-function;
