RHF76-052AM

RisingHF LoRaWAN module based on RHF76-052AM chip. It is a low power，low cost and small size module embedded with LoRa® chip SX1276 of Semtech, and ultra-low power MCU STM32L051/052xx of ST. The application of LoraWAN® module is targeted at wireless sensor network，AMR and others IoT devices powered by battery which need low power consumption to extend the battery life.

RHF76-052AM key features are listed below, details can be foun into datasheet:

  • Low power consumption: 1.45uA sleep current in WOR mode;
  • Dual band design:
    • Low frequency band: 434MHz/470MHz;
    • High frequency band: 868MHz/915MHz;
  • User-friendly interface:
    • AT commands;
    • SPI/USART/I2C/USB, 2 × ADC，10 × GPIOs;
  • Support LoRaWAN® protocol;
